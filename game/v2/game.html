HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to Critter Clash!

This is a 2 player die-based game. Players will each start with 100 HP, and can deal damage to the other player through rolling the 2 dice on screen. The sum of the dice roll will be the amount of damage your critter deals.

If a 1 is rolled, the player's turn will be skipped. If two 1's, or snake eyes, are rolled, the opposing player's HP will be reset to 100.

To win, please defeat the other player's critter first!
